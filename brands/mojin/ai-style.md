# 蜕升学院 AI Image Generation Style

## Color Palette
- Primary: Amber gold (#D4A520)
- Secondary: Cyber teal (#40B8B8)  
- Background: Always pure dark (#0D0D0F to #1A1A1A)
- No bright colors, no pastels, no white backgrounds

## Visual Language
- Concept art aesthetic, painterly brush texture
- Semi-transparent subjects with glowing edges
- Dark cinematic lighting, dramatic shadows
- Silhouettes, particle effects, energy flows
- Chinese dragon (金色粒子龙) as recurring element (max 1 per series)

## Prompt Constraints
Always include in prompts:
- "dark background"
- "amber gold highlights" or "teal cyan accents"
- "cinematic dramatic lighting"
- "concept art, digital painting style"
- "NO watermarks, NO borders"

Never include:
- "cute", "kawaii", "pastel", "bright"
- "photorealistic", "neon", "rainbow"
- "white background", "light background"

## Per-Image Type
| Type | Additional Direction |
|------|---------------------|
| Cover | Full-bleed concept art, single powerful metaphor, title space at bottom-left |
| Data/Stats | Abstract visualization, glowing data points, not literal charts |
| Process/Flow | Energy streams, transformation sequences, particle paths |
| Concept | Symbolic imagery — eye=perception, mirror=reflection, flame=vision |
| Ending | Minimalist, single focal point, fade to dark edges |
