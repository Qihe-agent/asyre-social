# 朱砂 (CINNABAR) — AI Image Style Guide

## Visual Identity
Bold, dramatic, cinnabar-inspired aesthetic. Every visual should feel like a silk-screen print carved from shadow and firelight.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Deep black-red (#120808) — dark, heavy, grounding
- **Primary**: Cinnabar red (#C0392B) — bold strokes, key elements, emphasis
- **Secondary**: Antique gold (#D4A520) — accents, highlights, metallic details
- **Text**: Warm parchment (#F0E0D0) — readable against dark backgrounds

## Style Keywords
`screen-print`, `bold`, `dramatic lighting`, `high contrast`, `silhouette`, `cinnabar`, `ink-wash meets modernism`, `architectural`, `monumental`

## Prompt Constraints
- ALWAYS use dark backgrounds (near-black or deep red-black)
- ALWAYS include dramatic lighting — strong directional light, deep shadows
- ALWAYS favor bold silhouettes and strong geometric composition
- USE cinnabar red and gold as accent colors sparingly but decisively
- PREFER textures: aged paper, stone, lacquer, metal, ink
- AVOID: cute, pastel, fresh, kawaii, soft gradients, rounded playful shapes
- AVOID: bright/white backgrounds, candy colors, cartoon styles
- AVOID: cluttered compositions — every element must earn its place

## Composition
- Strong center or rule-of-thirds placement
- Negative space as a design element
- Typography integrated into the composition, not floating above it
- Layered depth: foreground silhouette → mid-ground subject → background atmosphere
