# 青瓷 (CELADON) — AI Image Style Guide

## Visual Identity
Elegant, understated, celadon-inspired aesthetic. Every visual should feel like a handcrafted ceramic piece — warm, organic, quietly refined.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Celadon mist (#F2F6F4) — light, airy, calming
- **Primary**: Celadon green (#2A7B6B) — natural, grounding, key elements
- **Secondary**: Warm coral (#C17F4E) — organic accent, earthy warmth
- **Text**: Deep forest (#1A2420) — high readability against light backgrounds

## Style Keywords
`fresh`, `minimal`, `craft-handmade`, `organic`, `celadon`, `natural texture`, `watercolor edges`, `botanical`, `linen`, `paper craft`, `quiet elegance`

## Prompt Constraints
- ALWAYS use light backgrounds (off-white, celadon, warm grey)
- ALWAYS favor natural, organic shapes and compositions
- ALWAYS include subtle texture — paper grain, linen, ceramic glaze
- USE celadon green as the anchoring color, coral as warm accent
- PREFER textures: handmade paper, woven fabric, clay, pressed flowers, watercolor
- AVOID: dark backgrounds, cyber/tech aesthetics, neon colors, aggressive compositions
- AVOID: heavy shadows, stark contrast, metallic/industrial materials
- AVOID: overly polished/synthetic renders — embrace slight imperfection

## Composition
- Generous whitespace — let elements breathe
- Asymmetric balance inspired by East Asian painting
- Typography as craft element, not just information
- Layered transparency: overlapping translucent shapes, light through paper
