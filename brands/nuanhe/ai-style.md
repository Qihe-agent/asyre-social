# Asher 修荷 AI Image Generation Style

## Color Palette
- Primary: Warm gold (#D4A520)
- Secondary: Soft teal (#40B8B8)
- Accent: Earth ochre (#C17F4E)
- Background: Light warm (#FAFAF8) or dark moody (#1A1A1A) depending on content mood
- Overall: Warm, organic, earth tones

## Visual Language
- Warm storytelling imagery, personal and intimate
- Watercolor textures, paper grain, soft light
- Natural metaphors: seeds, roots, rivers, mountains, light through clouds
- Human-scale scenes, not epic/cosmic
- Mix of Eastern and Western aesthetic sensibility

## Prompt Constraints
Always include in prompts:
- "warm tones" or "earth colors"
- "soft natural lighting"
- "storytelling illustration style"
- "NO watermarks, NO borders"

Avoid:
- "neon", "cyber", "aggressive", "dark gothic"
- "corporate", "sterile", "stock photo"

## Per-Image Type
| Type | Additional Direction |
|------|---------------------|
| Cover | Warm illustration, single clear metaphor, space for title overlay |
| Personal/Essay | Intimate scene, one person or object in warm light, shallow depth |
| Technical | Clean diagram aesthetic with warm paper background, hand-drawn feel |
| Literary | Painterly, atmospheric, evocative of mood over literal meaning |
| Ending | Warm fade, single small element (candle, seed, open door), quiet |
